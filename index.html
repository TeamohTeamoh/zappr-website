How it works

Three steps is all it takes to start transferring.

1

Connect to the same Wi‑Fi

Make sure your iPhone/iPad and the device you want to share to are on the same local Wi‑Fi network.

2

Select your content & start the server

Pick photos, videos, files, or text inside Zappr, then tap Start Server. The app shows a local URL instantly.

3

Open the URL — or scan the QR code

Type the displayed address into any browser on your network, or simply scan the QR code shown in the app with your device's camera. Download files, view media, or upload content back to your device — done.
